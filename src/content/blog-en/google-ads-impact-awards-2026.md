## What are the Google Ads Impact Awards?

The Impact Awards are a global programme in which Google recognises marketers and agencies that make a real difference with AI-driven advertising. Winners are announced at Google Marketing Live — the annual event followed by marketers, agencies, and business leaders worldwide.

## Why this matters

For us, this nomination confirms what we see every day with our clients: AI makes the difference, but only when you know how to deploy it. Most companies have access to the same tools. The difference lies in the strategy behind them.

We don't use AI to do more — we use it to make smarter choices. Less waste, better targeting, sharper results. That's precisely what Google created this award to recognise.

## What this means for our clients

Nothing changes. We keep doing what we do. But it's good to know that the way we approach your campaigns is being recognised by the company that built the platform.
