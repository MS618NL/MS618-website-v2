## SEO is a growth strategy. Not a technical to-do list.

Most companies get SEO wrong. They treat it as a checklist: fix your titles, write meta descriptions, speed up the page. Those things matter — but they're outputs of a strategy, not the strategy itself.

At MS618, SEO is a growth engine. A systematic approach to getting the right people to your site, at the right moment in their buying journey, through organic search. Not as a one-off. Structurally.

## Our approach

### 1. Start with the market: search intent and competitive landscape

Before we touch a single page or fix a single technical issue, we understand the market. What are your buyers searching for? Where are they in the decision process? What intent sits behind those searches?

We map the full search landscape — from early-stage awareness to bottom-of-funnel buying intent. That map becomes the foundation for everything else.

### 2. Technical SEO audit

No strategy succeeds on a broken foundation. We dig into:

- Crawlability and indexation (nothing valuable getting missed)
- Core Web Vitals (LCP, CLS, INP — with direct ranking impact)
- Site structure and internal linking (how Google moves through your site)
- Duplicate content and canonicalisation
- Structured data / Schema.org implementation
- Mobile-first indexing

### 3. Content architecture

We design the content structure your site needs to own its topic area. Which pages? How do they connect? Which terms do you go after first?

We use a pillar-cluster model: authoritative pillar pages covering each core topic, backed by targeted cluster content that answers the specific questions your buyers are asking.

### 4. Content creation and optimisation

Content ranks when it fully satisfies search intent — not before. We write and optimise:

- Service pages that build credibility and drive enquiries
- Blog and knowledge articles that establish authority
- Landing pages for specific audience segments
- Case studies that do the selling for you

### 5. Link building and authority

Links remain one of the strongest ranking signals. But irrelevant links don't move the needle. We build quality, topically relevant links through:

- Thought leadership placements in trade publications
- Digital PR and newsjacking
- Partnerships and co-creation
- Competitor backlink analysis and link gap strategy
