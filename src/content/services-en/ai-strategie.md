## You know you need to do something with AI. But what?

Nine in ten SMEs know AI is changing their market. Almost no one knows where to start. The result: a sprawl of tools, isolated experiments, and the feeling of falling behind without knowing on what.

That is where we bring direction. Not by doing more, but by choosing exactly where AI makes a real difference for your business.

## What an AI strategy is

An AI strategy is a concrete plan for where and how you use AI to grow, instead of trying out isolated tools. It starts with your goals and processes, not the technology. The question is not "which tool is newest", but "where does time or revenue leak away, and can AI fix it".

## How we approach it

We have been AI-first since before ChatGPT. AI is not bolted onto our work as a trick, but built into every layer: strategy, content, visibility and advertising.

### 1. Mapping the opportunities

We look at where the biggest gains sit in your marketing and processes. Not a list of a hundred possibilities, but the few places where AI makes the difference now.

### 2. The right tools, not all tools

We pick the best AI per situation, not one tool for everything. Gemini, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Midjourney and more, depending on what the task needs. You get the result, we handle the technology.

### 3. Implement and embed

A strategy that does not get built is an opinion. We implement AI in your marketing and processes, and bring your team along so it keeps working.

## AI in visibility and growth

AI also changes how customers find you. More and more, your buyer asks ChatGPT or Perplexity a question instead of scrolling through Google. That is why AI strategy ties directly to [GEO optimization](/en/services/geo-optimalisatie/): making sure AI models reference your business in their answers.
