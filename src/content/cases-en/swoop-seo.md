## Who is Swoop

Swoop is an international marketing platform for outsourcing group transportation. A competitive market where visibility in Google directly translates into new customers, and where the competition differs per region and per service.

## The challenge

When we started, over three years ago, there was no SEO strategy. A strong platform and a growing market, but organic visibility lagged behind what the business was worth. The ask was not a quick fix, but a partner who builds SEO structurally and keeps it there.

## The approach

Not a one-off project, but an ongoing partnership of more than three years now:

- **A technical foundation that works with you**: crawlability, structure and speed in order
- **A content architecture that matches how customers search**, per service and per region
- **Continuous measurement and adjustment** on the terms that matter commercially, not on vanity metrics

## The result

Structurally better positions on the core terms, year after year. For Swoop, SEO has become a predictable channel instead of a gamble.
